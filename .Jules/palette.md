# Palette's Journal - Critical UX/Accessibility Learnings

## 2024-05-23 - PDF Accessibility Basics
**Learning:** In LaTeX-generated PDFs, explicit metadata (`pdftitle`, `pdfauthor`, `pdflang`) and clickable links (`\href`) are critical for accessibility and modern UX, but often overlooked in templates.
**Action:** Always check `\hypersetup` configuration and scan for raw text links (email, URLs) in LaTeX documents.
