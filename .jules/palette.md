## 2026-01-03 - PDF Accessibility Basics
**Learning:** Even static PDFs benefit from "invisible" UX. Adding `pdftitle`, `pdfauthor`, and `pdflang` to `\hypersetup` makes the document friendlier to screen readers and window titles. Wrapping text URLs in `\href` is a zero-cost usability win.
**Action:** Always check `\hypersetup` configuration in LaTeX projects and ensure contact info is interactive.
